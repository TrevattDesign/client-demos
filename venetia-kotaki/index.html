About Dr Kotaki

Helping people is more than
just a career for me

Therapy for me is a safe and confidential place where people can be themselves without fear of judgement — where they can feel listened to, understood, and supported, even during their darkest moments.

I am a Doctor of Counselling Psychology, a Chartered Counselling Psychologist registered with the BPS, and a Practitioner Psychologist registered with the HCPC. My clinical career spans NHS primary, secondary and in-patient settings, as well as private practice.

I work with adults, couples and families, using CBT, Psychoanalytic and Psychodynamic therapies, Humanistic and Integrative approaches — always tailored to what you need. I also offer sessions in Greek.

My Approach

An individualised programme
built around you

01

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy

A structured, evidence-based approach that helps you understand the links between thoughts, feelings and behaviour — and change the patterns that aren't working.

02

Psychoanalytic & Psychodynamic

Exploring how past experiences and unconscious processes shape your present — creating deeper self-understanding and lasting change.

03

Humanistic Therapy

Centred on your innate capacity for growth and self-awareness, with warmth, empathy and unconditional positive regard at the core of our work together.

04

Integrative Approach

Drawing flexibly from multiple modalities, I create a personalised treatment plan based on your needs, goals and what the evidence shows works best for your difficulties.
