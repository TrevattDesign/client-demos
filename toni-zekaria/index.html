About Toni

A counsellor who understands that life rarely goes to plan

Before training as a counsellor, I spent years in careers built entirely around people. I managed a luxury boutique hotel, and then started and ran my own bakery and cafe. Both taught me a great deal about care, communication, and what people need when things get hard.

That background shapes how I work. I bring warmth, curiosity, and a genuine interest in the person in front of me to every session. I'm not here to hand out advice or tell you what to do. My role is to help you understand yourself more clearly, so that the choices you make can come from a more grounded, considered place.

I offer counselling in English and French and work with adults of all ages. Sessions are available in person at an NHS GP surgery in Hendon, NW4, as well as online and by phone. I am registered with the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP) and work fully within their ethical framework, with regular supervision and ongoing professional development.

How I work

Therapy that meets you where you are

I take an integrative approach, which means I draw on a range of methods and adapt how I work to suit what you actually need. No fixed script, no one-size-fits-all framework.

Flexible, integrative approach

Rather than following a single method, I draw from a range of evidence-based approaches and tailor each session to you. Your needs are the starting point, not a theory.

Short-term or open-ended

Counselling can be brief and focused on a specific issue, or longer and more exploratory. We will work out together what makes sense for you, and revisit that as we go.

A space that is genuinely yours

Every session is 50 minutes, confidential, and free from judgement. Whatever you bring, I will meet it with respect for your individuality, your background, and your own way of making sense of the world.

Individual and couples sessions

I work with individuals working through personal difficulties, and with couples who want to improve communication, reconnect, or navigate conflict together. Sessions are available in person, online, or by phone.
